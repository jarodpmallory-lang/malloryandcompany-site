Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Where do your products come from?

We source products from vetted dropship suppliers and fulfill orders directly from the supplier to your door. Every product we carry is hand-picked based on real trend data — TikTok virality, Amazon bestseller rankings, and verified customer reviews. If it's in our store, it earned its place.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. Click the link in that email to track your package in real time. If you don't see a tracking email within 3–5 business days of ordering, check your spam folder or email us with your order number.

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery on items in their original, unused condition. The buyer is responsible for return shipping unless the item arrived damaged or defective. Email us at support@malloryandcompany.com to start a return. Full details on our Returns page.
