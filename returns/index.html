Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date. After 30 days, items are not eligible for return or exchange unless they arrived damaged or defective.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it — unused, unaltered, and in its original packaging. Items that show signs of use, have been assembled, or are missing original packaging may not qualify for a full refund.

How to Start a Return

To initiate a return or exchange, email us at support@malloryandcompany.com with the following information:

  • Your order number
  • The item(s) you want to return or exchange
  • The reason for the return
  • Photos of the item(s) if they arrived damaged or defective

We'll respond within 1–2 business days with instructions on how to ship your item back to us.

Do not ship items back before receiving confirmation from our team. Returns sent without prior authorization may not be processed.

Return Shipping

The buyer is responsible for return shipping costs, unless the item arrived damaged, defective, or was incorrectly sent. We recommend using a trackable shipping method — we cannot be responsible for items lost in transit on their way back to us.

If an item arrived damaged or defective, contact us immediately and we will arrange a prepaid return label or send a replacement at no charge.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days (processing times vary by bank or card issuer).

Refunds cover the original item price. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Exchanges

We accept exchanges on items that meet the same eligibility requirements as returns — unused, in original condition, within 30 days of delivery.

To request an exchange, email support@malloryandcompany.com and let us know what item you'd like to exchange for. If the replacement item has a different price, we'll handle the difference via your original payment method or a payment link.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned due to hygiene, safety, or product nature:

  • Personal care and beauty products that have been opened or used
  • Consumable items
  • Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase
  • Digital products or downloads
  • Gift cards

Have a question about a return?

Email us and we'll get back to you within 1–2 business days.
